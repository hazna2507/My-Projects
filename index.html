Case Studies

The Mid-Century Evolution

A dark, compartmentalized 1960s mid-century home that felt completely disconnected from the landscape.

We stripped back the non-load-bearing partitions to create a singular, light-filled great room and re-engineered the ceiling grid to conceal HVAC systems.

We introduced wide-plank European oak to bring warmth to the expansive, concrete-framed volume, complemented by a minimalist palette of honed travertine and matte black steel accents.
